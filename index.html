About Us

We are group of yoga lovers who are offering yoga retreats in Croatia. All inclusive; body, mind and soul holidays at heart of Istria. We are making real our dream to combine our gifts and create an offering of heart-felt yoga and food in a beautiful space area of Bale which we would often visit when in Croatia.

Our guests are invited to leave behind their everyday lives, take some time to nourish and recharge their body and soul, and to reconnect with themselves and with new friends too! In the spectacular surroundings of Istria, you will find peace and deep relaxation. Practice and explore Yoga and Meditation with Ksenija, eat Tom’s delicious Vegan food, spend time with like minded people or escape for some alone time!You will also have free time to rest, wander the surrounding area, take time for yourself and even detox in the infrared sauna!

Meet the team

Ksenija Brajkovic Yoga teacher

"In 2020, I became the founder and director of Wallet friendly yoga by Xena. While growing up in Croatia, all I could envision for myself was living life in the sunshine, under the palm trees. Over 10 years of soul-searching I explored city life, but I have found my dream life by the beach in Croatia which I want to share with our guests. Because of my own experience, I believe that everyone can live their dreams. To do this, our work is to show up for ourselves, to become clear and open to receive the gifts we all deserve. My Yogic Life started when I finished course in Bali specialised for Vinyasa Flow and Yin Yoga. I believe that a yogic life is wholistic and all-inclusive: body, mind, and spirit. My classes are heartfelt and intuitive, gently challenging and deeply nourishing.

Through creating a safe space and emphasis on softening and synchronising the breath I help you flow with more ease and grace while deepening your practice. I love to share pieces of wisdom that speak directly to you, so you can bring them home as an inspiration for your own life. So you can learn to relax and trust more easily. During this retreat I will provide 2 yoga classes per day and meditation for my guests."

Tomislav Brajkovic, chef

Tomislav Brajkovic, our private chef in villa. His background in the culinary arts began with instruction from his mother. After finishing scool for chef in 2001. he worked in many prestige restaurants in Ireland. For a week he will cook healthy authentic meals with vegan option for our guests.

"Without a doubt, what I love most about being a personal chef is when my clients tell me they can feel the love and care that went into what I prepared for them and what a positive difference I make in their lives."Tom

Drazen Modric, personal driver and tour guide

Drazen is our personal driver and tour guide. He have specialized in designing and managing tours in Croatia. If you’re looking for a croatian private tour guide able to combine knowledge, experience, local insight and a barrel-load of fun along the way then he is best person! He graduated in Tourism Management and by combining his professional training with passion for this part of the world, he is able to offer our guests tours that are both reliable and filled with genuine excitement. During this retreat he will be driving 9 seats van and bringing group to excursions.
"People don’t take trips, trips take people."Drazen
